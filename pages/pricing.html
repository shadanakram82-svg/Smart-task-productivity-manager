Pricing FAQs

Everything you need to know about billing and subscriptions.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also support PayPal and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the prorated difference will be applied to your next billing cycle.

Are there any setup costs or hidden fees?

No, there are no hidden fees or setup costs. The price you see on the pricing page is exactly what you pay. Enterprise plans may have custom implementation fees.
